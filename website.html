Q

How much water should I drink daily?

Adults should aim for 8-10 glasses (64-80 oz) of water daily, more if you're active or in hot weather. Your hydration needs may vary based on climate, activity level, and overall health.

Q

How many hours of sleep do I need?

Most adults need 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night for optimal health and well-being. Sleep quality is just as important as quantity for cognitive function and physical recovery.
